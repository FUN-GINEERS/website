PUTT PUTT BOOM
LUDUM DARE 41

Design: Kevin Kohler (Bobgar), Daniel Shaffron (Discount Llama), & Andrew Wilkinson (Android)
Programming: Kevin Kohler (Bobgar) & Orion Crocker (Kleiner)
2D Art: Jason Patrick (Frothy)

Made for Ludum Dare 41 (Combine 2 Incompatible Genres) Put Put Boom is a tower-defense mini-golf mash up. Build and upgrade towers while fending off the balls of hordes of golfers.

Score is accumulated by the number of swings club members took to beat the hole (they give up at 8). Money is $400 to start, then every wave's score X 10.

STACK THIEF
LUDUM DARE 40

Created by Bobgar, Android, Kleiner, Discount Llama, McStabbyFace, & Frothy

Navigate Stealie Willie through a house to steal as many items as you can before time runs out! Get a bonus multiplier for delivering tall stacks of items to your sack, but don't drop them! W, A, S, D - Moves the character. Q, E - attempts to re-balance items on your arms. Mouse1 - Click items and drag them onto your stack of stuff.
